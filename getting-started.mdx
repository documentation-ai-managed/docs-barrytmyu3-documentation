## Quick Start

YTMEC provides a comprehensive platform for exploring Traditional Chinese Medicine. You begin your journey by creating an account, setting up your profile, and navigating key sections. Follow these steps to access courses, resources, and community features quickly.

<Callout kind="tip">
  Prepare your email address and a strong password before starting. The entire signup process takes under 2 minutes.
</Callout>

## Create an Account and Set Up Your Profile

Use the guided signup flow to get started.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit YTMEC" icon="globe">
    Open your browser and go to `https://ytmec.com/signup`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details" icon="user">
    Provide your email, create a password, and select your experience level (Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification link from `no-reply@ytmec.com` and click it to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Profile" icon="settings">
    Add your name, preferred language (English or Traditional Chinese), and interests like Acupuncture or Herbal Medicine.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tips for First-Time Users

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="Customize Notifications" default-open="true">
    Go to Settings `{}` > Notifications to select updates on new courses or forum replies.
  </Expandable>
  <Expandable title="Bookmark Content">
    Click the star icon on any lesson to save it to your dashboard.
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>

<Callout kind="info">
  Mobile users: Download the PWA by adding `https://ytmec.com` to your home screen for app-like access.
</Callout>
