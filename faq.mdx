## Account and Access Issues

Use these solutions for login problems or account setup.

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="How do I create an account?" default-open="true">

Follow these steps to sign up:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Registration" icon="user-plus">
    Go to the YTMEC homepage and click `Sign Up` in the top-right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details" icon="edit-3">
    Provide your email, create a strong password, and accept the terms.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification link and click it to activate.
  </Step>
</Steps>

  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="What if I forgot my password?">

<Callout kind="info">
  Reset your password securely without losing access to your saved TCM study notes.
</Callout>

1. Click `Forgot Password?` on the login page.
2. Enter your registered email.
3. Follow the reset link sent to your inbox (check spam if needed).

  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>
